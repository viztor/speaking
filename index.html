演讲，没那么容易.

一场大会之上，或是公司会议之时，甚至是在车上试图 说服警官不要罚款，因为你三倍超速还在平板电脑上看 一周立波秀的时候，在别人面前讲话是在可以称 作是令人恐慌的体验了。

发什么什么？

  • 我是谁？ I'm Zach Holman and I work for GitHub. I've spoken at more technical conferences than anyone else on the planet, aside from those who have spoken at more conferences than me. Follow me on Twitter if you're into that kind of thing, weirdo.
  • 你是谁？ I wrote this with conference speakers in mind, but the ability to convey information with your mouth is really something we can all improve upon. Presenting a PowerPoint to your boss ain't all too much different than a conference, for example.
  • 这些大概要多少钱？ Make your checks payable to a shell corporation I have in the Caymans. Just kidding; it's all free. I hope you enjoy it. Also, if I ever see one of your talks, it better be damn good.
